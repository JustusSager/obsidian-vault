# `ReLu`

$$
\begin{align}
  ReLu(x) = \left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
      x \quad \text{if $x \gt 0$}\\
      0 \quad \text{otherwise}
    \end{array}
  \right.
\end{align}
$$

## derivative
$$
\begin{align}
  ReLu_{derivative}(x) = \left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
      1 \quad \text{if $x \gt 0$}\\
      0 \quad \text{otherwise}
    \end{array}
  \right.
\end{align}
$$
